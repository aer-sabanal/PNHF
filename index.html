Barangay Hinipaan

Hinipaan is a barangay in the municipality of Mercedes, in the province of Camarines Norte. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 2,325. This represented 4.20% of the total population of Mercedes.

The Hinipaan Falls

Hinipaan Falls is located in Barangay Hinipaan Mercedes, Camarines Norte. This waterfall is one of the tourist attractions here in Mercedes. The height of this waterfall is approximately 8 meters. If you're from Daet and want to go to Hinipaan Falls, go to Daet Central Terminal or Camambugan Terminal and ride on a jeep to byaheng Pambuhan. This will take 20 to 30 minutes to arrive in Hinipaan. Go to the barangay hall and inquire about a tour guide; the fee for the tour guide is ₱250-300. After you pay, the tour guide will take you to Hinipaan Resort, and you will pass through it before trekking to Hinipaan Falls for 1 and a half to 2 hours.

Nearby Tourist Attractions

Hinipaan Resort

It is located at Brgy. Hinipaan, Mercedes, Camarines Norte.The HINIPAAN RIVER is only 100 meters away from Brgy. Hall of Hinipaan. It offers cottages for rent right next to the river. The entrance fee is very affordable and accessible sometimes free. The river has lots of fish and you will enjoy the peaceful place and clear cold water.

Colasi Falls

Colasi Falls is about 70 ft high with 3 major-cascading falls along the stretch and the mountain abounds with lush tropical vegetations with migratory and domestic birds. It is accessible by land about one and a half hour from the Municipality of Daet going to Brgy. Colasi and from there, about two hours of trek which virtually adds the thrill and adventure.
